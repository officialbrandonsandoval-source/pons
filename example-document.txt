PONS AI Assistant - Feature Overview

PONS is an advanced personal AI assistant designed to help you manage your digital life, integrate your personal data, and provide intelligent insights.

Key Features:

1. Personal Integrations
   - Connect social media accounts (Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook)
   - Link financial accounts via Plaid
   - Sync calendar and email
   - Aggregate health and fitness data

2. Document Knowledge Base
   - Upload documents to create a personal knowledge base
   - Ask questions about your documents using natural language
   - Vector-based semantic search for relevant information
   - Supports TXT, MD, and JSON formats

3. AI-Powered Insights
   - Analyze social media presence and engagement
   - Track financial health and spending patterns
   - Generate personalized recommendations
   - Identify trends and patterns in your data

4. Smart Task Management
   - Schedule meetings and events
   - Manage CRM contacts and leads
   - Create and organize content
   - Set reminders and follow-ups

5. Privacy & Security
   - All data stored securely in Supabase
   - OpenAI API for AI processing
   - Local storage for preferences
   - No data shared with third parties

Getting Started:
1. Configure your integrations in Settings
2. Upload documents to Vault
3. Start chatting with PONS in Copilot
4. Ask questions about your documents or request insights

Example Questions:
- "What are the key features of PONS?"
- "Analyze my social media presence"
- "What's in my documents about integrations?"
- "Give me financial insights"
- "Schedule a meeting for tomorrow"

PONS learns from your data to provide increasingly personalized and helpful assistance over time.
