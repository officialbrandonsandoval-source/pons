## Privacy
- Voice processing happens in your browser
- No audio is sent to servers
- Only the transcribed text is sent to OpenAI for processing

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone not working?
- Check browser permissions
- Make sure microphone access is allowed
- Try refreshing the page

### Voice not being recognized?
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Check your internet connection
- Try using Chrome for best results

### AI not speaking responses?
- Make sure the speaker icon is enabled (green)
- Check your device volume
- Ensure Speech Synthesis is supported in your browser
